Everybody had matching towels

It wasn't a rock

A short book of original poetry by Baltimore-based poet Patrick Swickard on a variety of topics.

Bar fights. Stairway accidents. Giraffes. Horseshoe crabs. Oysters. Holidays ruined. Bee people. Elephants. And more.
