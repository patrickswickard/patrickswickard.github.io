Darkly humorous short stories, poetry, and other oddities by Baltimore-based writer Patrick Swickard. Featuring time travel, Mormon cyberpunk noir, body horror, fleshed out nightmares, conceptual short fiction, more Hitlers than you can shake a stick at, and other weirdness. An occasionally unwelcome glimpse into the open sewer of the author’s mind, presented by the publisher with his full apologies.
