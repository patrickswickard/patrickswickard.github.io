Second volume of poetry by Hoosier poet Bryan Trackwell, with photos and illustrations by Kyle Trackwell.

Antlers. Ol’ Brown. Late night hours. Lilith’s garden. Moonlit cove. Hellhound. Cage. Comedy and tragedy. Essence of red. Nemesis. Karma. The black cat. Scarecrow. And more.
