A word cloud is a visualization technique which shows the most commonly used words in a text (aside from short words like “a” and “the” and “and”) in a convenient format where the font size corresponds to the frequency the word is used. It’s one way of quickly seeing the content of a piece of text. This book features word cloud visualizations (generated by Python) for numerous pieces of specifically Taylor Swift-themed erotica dredged from the depths of the internet. Dozens of one-handed reads featuring America’s Sweetheart, conveniently summarized for your prurient enjoyment!
