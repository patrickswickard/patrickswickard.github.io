Third book of poetry by Hoosier poet Bryan D. “Red” Trackwell. This one is pretty raunchy, folks.

Carbonites. Trailer trash. Slumlords. Mama tried. Hoarder. Mail order brides. Kruzan. Praise the Lord. High class trash. False advertising. Family values. Grimace. The prowl. And more.
