You know exactly what you're getting into when you start watching this. It's going to be full of spraying bodily fluids, people vomiting sex slugs with their eyes rolled back into their heads, un-sexy sex scenes, ugly people fucking and fighting, Canadians, all the reasons you tune in.

You've never been to an orgy. Not a zombie orgy, not a regular orgy. Nothing too extreme, more along the lines of Cirque du Soleil than dongs going in, though you weren't sitting close enough to confirm any penetration or lack thereof. No blue men at least.
